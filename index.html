Retro Room Architect

Sketch out an isometric hangout inspired by classic hotel builders.

Facing: North-East
Hover over the room to inspect tiles.

Palette

Pick a floor style or furnish the space. Left click to place, right click to remove furniture. Switch to walk mode to send the avatar exploring.

Controls

  • Left click Place the selected tile or furniture (build mode)
  • Walk Mode button Toggle avatar controls, then left click to send them walking
  • Right click Remove furniture from the hovered tile
  • R / Shift + R Rotate hovered furniture or cycle the selection (Shift reverses)
  • Shift + click Sample the hovered floor style
  • Zoom buttons Use + and − to adjust your view of the room

Built as a playground for experimenting with isometric room design.
